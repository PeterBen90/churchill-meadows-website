Churchill Meadows Community

Look at what the community has to offer!

Churchill Meadows Community Page

Churchill Meadows offers a very family orientated, suburban way of life. A noticeably quiet neighborhood finds many residents enjoying their time at home or amongst the many parks situated throughout the community. Churchill Meadows Library boasts a modern facility equipped with a dedicated multi-purpose room, shared triple gymnasium, full size studio and Recreation & Parks programming.

Residents also have ease of access to the much improved Erin Mills Town Centre, the state of the art Erin Meadows Community Centre located on Erin Centre Boulevard, and the future Churchill Meadows Community Centre & Park. Churchill Meadows real estate also offers access to surrounding entertainment including the ever popular Toronto Premium Outlets in neighboring Milton, the one of a kind experience at the 5-Drive In movie theater, and a 5 minute drive from the historic Village of Streetsville; offering countless unique eating experiences, cafes, and pubs such as The Franklin House. The at home suburban atmosphere of Churchill Meadows combined with access to multiple commercial retailers, major shopping centre’s, and family fun has made Churchill Meadows real estate a top destination for families looking to buy into Mississauga.

Community Entertainment

Churchill Meadows Library:

Those who have purchased Churchill Meadows real estate will enjoy this modern community facility that acts as a community library, as well as the school library for St. Joan of Arc Secondary School. This facility boasts amenities such as an Active Living Centre with a dedicated multi-purpose room, shared triple gymnasium and full-size dance studio and a skate park. Recreation & Parks programming through the facility includes fencing, yoga, tai chi, kickboxing, karate, skateboarding, volleyball, badminton, basketball, tennis, little tykes soccer, floor hockey and more.The Churchill Meadows Library can be located at 3801 Thomas St. and is open Monday-Thursday 8:00AM - 9:00PM, Friday 8:00AM - 6:00PM, Saturday 9:00AM - 5:00PM and Sunday 1:00PM - 5:00PM.

Erin Mills Town Center:

Erin Mills Town Centre is Mississauga’s second largest shopping centre, with over 185 stores and local services including Wal-Mart, LCBO, Loblaws and Rona. Fresh off of a $100 million redevelopment, the newly renovated complex features an expanded food court, sleek contemporary design, and an architecturally striking giant glass sphere. Residents of Churchill Meadows have extraordinarily easy access to the mall entrances from either Erin Centre Boulevard, or Eglinton Avenue, both which run parallel each other and extend through the entirety of Churchill Meadows.


Erin Mills Community Center:

Another advantage of buying Churchill Meadows real estate is the Erin Meadows Community Centre, just across the street on the east side of Erin Mills Town Centre. This state-of-the art building houses a 25-metre swimming pool, multi-function activity studio, and a public library complete with internet access, and activity programs for all ages. However, it should be noted to any prospective buyer of Churchill Meadows real estate that the official Churchill Meadows Community Centre & Park is currently under construction, slated for completion in the Fall of 2020. Churchill Meadows residents can find Erin Meadows Community Centre at 2800 Erin Centre Blvd & enjoy the usage of the centre between Monday to Friday: 7:30 am - 10:30 pm Saturday to Sunday: 7:30 am - 9:00 pm.

Churchill Meadows Community Centre & Park:

Amazing news came to the community of Churchill Meadows in late 2014 when the city council of Mississauga approved providing a new park and Community Centre within the Ninth Line lands future development area between Highway 403 and 401. The Churchill Meadows Community Centre & Park is a 75,000 square foot, $51 million approved landmark project including series of domes, artificial turfs, trails, and an ultra modern Community Centre. The project will serve the urban densification along the City's western boundary of Churchill Meadows real estate, with a recreation centre and a 50-acre park.

Toronto Premium Outlets:

The fun continues for residents of Churchill Meadows when you consider how easily you can get to the Toronto Premium Outlets, located in neighboring Milton. Residents can take Ninth Line directly to Steeles or opt for the more scenic ride along Derry to Trafalgar in order to arrive at the Premium Outlet Centre. Opening in 2013 Toronto Premium Outlets is the first of its kind in Canada, boasting 400,000 square feet of shopping including the first ever Hudson's Bay Discount Store, which was recently replaced by Saks Fifth Avenue. The popularity of the outlet style shopping & many designer stores has increased year after year, proof of that is in the recent construction of a 5-storey parking facility to accommodate all travelers to the shopping centre. Being situated in a more remote, commercial area, residents of Churchill Meadows have the easiest and most direct access to the outlets of anyone in Mississauga. Outdoor food trucks, events, and great bargains put on at the Outlets are just more reasons to buy Churchill Meadows real estate.

Streetsville:

For a more local and unique experience, we find many residents of Churchill Meadows venturing into the historic town of Streetsville! Streetsville is a neighbourhood located in the northwestern corner of Mississauga, approximately a 6 to 8 minute drive from the heart of Churchill Meadows. Residents of Churchill Meadows will experience one of a kind experiences held in Streetsville such as the Bread and Honey Festival, the annual Mississauga Santa Clause Parade, and the Christmas Market. The Bread and Honey festival become an annual event being held on the first weekend of June. The festival is held at Streetsville Memorial Park. Some of the events include live music performances, a carnival run, a consumers market, a pancake breakfast, and parade. After a 2 year renovation the Streetsville Village Square now includes a covered stage, the town cenotaph and outdoor seating for all. The Christmas Market includes food and gift vendors, live entertainment and the lighting of the towns Christmas Tree! We find that when you buy Churchill Meadows real estate, you essentially afford yourself and your family the opportunity to experience and enjoy some of the greatest experiences Mississauga has to offer all within an arm's reach.

Lifestyle

Churchill Meadows the right fit for you?
Find out what we have to say about who lives in the community.

  • Pros

    Churchill Meadows is a fantastic neighbourhood for those seeking a suburban lifestyle. Filled with parks and greenery, Churchill Meadows embodies a textbook family oriented neighbourhood. Churchill Meadows real estate offers the unique opportunity to buy into the neighbourhood with a starter townhome and progress your way to owning a beautiful luxury detach home within the same community!

  • Cons

    Churchill Meadows is not best suited for those seeking a busy city life, or single individuals. Although there are plenty of eateries and near by businesses, Churchill Meadows may not provide the immediate entertainment for those seeking an avid nightlife.

  • Schools

    Churchill Meadows offers some of the highest ranked schools within Mississauga. Some of which include elementary schools Churchill Meadows Public School and Oscar Peterson Public School. Middle schools Ruth Thompson, Erin Centre Middle School & High schools Stephen F Lewis Secondary and St. Joan of Arc Secondary School.

  • Transit

    Being situated on the Western border of Mississauga, residents have easy access to highways 403 and 401, as well as the 407 toll highway. Residents who rely heavily on Mississauga's MiWay bus system will be pleasantly pleased with the access to central bus routes, and a direct bus to the Erin Mills Town Centre bus terminal. There are also 3 GO Bus Stations relatively close to Churchill Meadows; Erin Mills GO being the closest while Streetsville GO, Meadowvale GO and Lisgar Stations also within 10 minutes.
