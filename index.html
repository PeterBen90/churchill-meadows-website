Churchill Meadows
Real Estate

Churchill Meadows is a newly developed, thriving community located in the western most area of Mississauga, bordering Milton to the west and Oakville to the South West. Churchill Meadows real estate offers a variety of housing opportunity from low rise condominiums to luxury estates, with the ability to BUY or RENT. This variety of affordable housing combined with exceptional location to major highways makes Churchill Meadows real estate extremely desirable for all walks of life.

Community Entertainment

Churchill Meadows offers a very family orientated, suburban way of life. A noticeably quiet neighborhood finds many residents enjoying their time at home or amongst the many parks situated throughout the community. Churchill Meadows Library boasts a modern facility equipped with a dedicated multi-purpose room, shared triple gymnasium, full size studio and Recreation & Parks programming.

Lifestyle

Churchill Meadows is best suited for families and young couples who will enjoy the outdoors and ambience of a suburban lifestyle. Those seeking Churchill Meadows real estate will find affordable living in the western part of Mississauga with close distances to everything, and the unique opportunity to progress from your first starter home to a 3500 square foot luxury estate all within the same community.
